			===================================================
				 Looping OR Iterative OR Repeatative Statements
			===================================================
=>The Purpose of  Looping OR Iterative OR Repeatative Statements is that "To Performs Certain Operation Repeatedly For Finite Number of Times until Test Condition becomes False."
=>In Python Programming, we have 2 Types of  Looping OR Iterative OR Repeatative Statements. They are

				1. while loop OR while..else loop
				2. for loop  OR  for..else loop

=>To work with Loops, we Must the Following Steps.
			
			1. Initlization Part  (Where to Start)
			2. Conditional Part (Where to Stop)
			3. Updation Part ( Incrementation OR Decrementation --Moving Forward and Backward)
